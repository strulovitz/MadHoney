# Payment Mechanisms — Reference Card

**Standalone reference for outreach. Full version embedded in `prologue.md` ("How To Pay Me Without Awkwardness").**

The wire-transfer ask in *Mad Honey* is not blocked by any structural problem. Every Big AI company already has finance and legal mechanisms in routine use that produce a one-time lump-sum payment to an external party with a clean tax code, a clean paper trail, and a defensible internal narrative. The list below catalogues them.

The amount is the same in every case. Pick the one that fits your culture.

---

## 💼 Acquisition-style mechanisms

1. **Acqui-hire (without the actual hiring).** Cash up front, vesting waived. Precedent: OpenAI ↔ Steinberger, Meta ↔ Schlicht. Tax code: business combination / employee compensation.

2. **Asset purchase — GitHub repository as IP.** Buyer acquires the codebase as intangible asset; seller's obligation is to take down all original copies. Tax code: intangible asset / capex.

3. **Trade-secret purchase.** Implementation details, unpublished test logs, operating know-how, next-iteration research direction. Tax code: intangible asset.

4. **Publishing-rights acquisition.** Acquire rights to *The Distributed AI Revolution* and *Mad Honey*; let them go out of print. Tax code: content acquisition.

5. **Shell-company M&A.** Inventor incorporates a Delaware/Cayman holding co; buyer acquires the shell. Tax code: business combination. **Cleanest paper trail.**

---

## 🛡️ Disclosure / safety-research mechanisms

6. **Critical-tier responsible-disclosure award (scaled bug bounty).** Publicly celebrated. Press-positive. Tax code: security expenditure. **Most defensible in the press cycle.**

7. **AI-safety research grant.** Grant to inventor's research foundation in a non-American jurisdiction. Tax code: research / philanthropic disbursement (often tax-deductible).

---

## ⚖️ Legal / settlement mechanisms

8. **Confidential pre-litigation buyout.** Settles all current and future claims (real or potential). Tax code: legal expense. **Most discreet.**

9. **NDA signing bonus + perpetual cooperation restriction.** Legal artifact justifying the payment. Tax code: protective legal expenditure.

---

## 🎤 Advisory / engagement mechanisms

10. **One-time strategic-advisory engagement.** Single deliverable (architectural review / workshop / keynote). Paid in full up front. Tax code: professional services.

11. **Distinguished-Fellow / endowed-chair appointment.** One-year stipend, no deliverables, no ongoing relationship. Tax code: fellowship grant.

---

## 🏛️ Government / contractor-routed mechanisms

12. **Defense-contract sub-routing.** Sub-contract under existing DARPA / Pentagon / IARPA program for "advanced distributed-architecture concepts." Available to firms with defense touchpoints (Palantir-adjacent, Bedrock defense lines, etc.). Tax code: government-contract sub-disbursement.

---

## 🏆 Selection guide

| Goal | Best mechanism |
|------|----------------|
| Maximum public defensibility, positive press cycle | **#6 Bug bounty (scaled)** |
| Maximum discretion, no public footprint | **#8 Pre-litigation buyout + #9 NDA signing bonus** |
| Cleanest accounting paper trail | **#5 Shell-company M&A** |
| Most precedent within the industry | **#1 Acqui-hire** |
| Charitable / philanthropic narrative | **#7 AI-safety research grant** |
| Defense-contractor companies specifically | **#12 Defense sub-routing** |

---

## The terminal outcome (every mechanism produces the same)

- One-time wire to multiple non-American banks of inventor's choosing
- Multiple jurisdictions, multiple currencies
- Repositories come down (`KillerBee`, `GiantHoneyBee`, related)
- Books go out of print
- Inventor disappears as a public entity
- Threat is closed

The constraint is not the mechanism. The constraint is the wire-transfer button.
