## Drone Communication Bandwidth Limitations
- Contested environments: as low as 600 bps in crucial bands
- Jamming: broadband noise, narrowband, GPS spoofing ($8 devices), protocol-aware
- Counter-measures: FHSS (2000+ hops/sec), SDR, MIMO, cognitive radio, redundant links
- Jamming causes high bit error rates, packet loss, loss of control
