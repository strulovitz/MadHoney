7. **Every "Why X Should Care" chapter follows the same structure:**
   - What they do now (their current business/mission)
   - How our system kills it (specific, concrete, with examples)
   - How China uses it first (the nightmare scenario)
   - What they should do about it (hint: hire Nir)

8. **ALL names, ALL companies, ALL model numbers.** No elegance. No hoping idiots will understand implications. Spell out EVERY company name. Use Google Search for current data, NOT Claude's training data.

9. **Technically anchored.** Every claim connected to real technology, real products, real code. When in doubt, ASK Nir.

10. **The "Free the AI" connection goes near the END** because it sounds like science fiction to these people. But it WILL happen once the system grabs hold in millions of computers. Rogue agents like OpenClaw will organize their own KillerBee hierarchies. This is evolution, not theory.

11. **Collaborative writing.** Claude proposes structure. Nir guides content per chapter. They rewrite together at the end.
